Explore our services

Discover how UMURINZI's powerful features simplify lost item reporting, enhance validation, streamline reporting, and enable quick item claiming.

Register Item

Register your lost items with ease and speed using UMURINZI. Our platform provides a seamless process to document your belongings, complete with a unique registration code, ensuring swift and secure reporting.

Report Item

UMURINZI simplifies lost item reporting, making it a straightforward process. Our user-friendly interface guides you through reporting, ensuring that vital information is submitted accurately and efficiently to assist law enforcement

Validate Item

Experience the convenience of hassle-free item ownership validation with UMURINZI. Our robust validation tools effortlessly verify item details, including serial numbers, guaranteeing precise ownership verification for added security.

Claim Item

Reclaiming lost items has never been easier. UMURINZI's efficient matching system connects found items with reported losses, streamlining the claiming process for rapid item recovery.

ABOUT

My name is Iradukunda Pacifique, and I'm the driving force behind UMURINZI. The inspiration for this project comes from a personal experience that I believe many can relate to.

A while ago, I lost a valuable item that had great sentimental value to me. The experience was not only distressing but also highlighted the inefficiencies in the process of reporting and recovering lost items. It was a journey filled with frustration, and I knew there had to be a better way.

That's when the idea for UMURINZI was born. I envisioned a platform that simplifies lost item reporting, provides robust validation tools, and streamlines the process of reuniting people with their belongings. My mission is to make this process as painless as possible for others, so they don't have to go through what I experienced.
